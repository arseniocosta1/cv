Selfie

I was born in 1984 in Torreira, Aveiro District in Portugal, living in Braga since 2005, where I moved to study at the University. My passion for Software Development started early in 1998 when I started learning C++ by myself so that I could create my own video-games. It was obvious to me that I should focus my education on Informatics. In 2004 I finished high school with a Level III Professional degree and next year I started a bachelor's degree in Computer Sciences. I have always been eager to learn and improve my technical skills, adopting the best practice and technologies.

Nowadays i am a senior full-stack developer with experience in most of the modern technologies, delivering great value in the whole software lifecycle.

What I Do

Right after graduating, I started an internship as a Software Developer in a startup company. That time I was responsible to design, implement and maintain a mobile messaging SMPP gateway. Due to the great results of my internship, I was hired to continue the project and start a new one. In 2013 I was challenged to start a research scholarship at the School of Medicine - University of Minho. The main goal of this scholarship was to design and develop a mobile application to promote healthy behaviors on a target group of individuals, but I also had the chance to work on other projects using web technologies. Since 2014, I am a member of the IT team of School of Medicity at the University of Minho, where I am responsible for the design, development, and maintenance of the school intranet portal.
