Selfie

I've started my professional career as an intern in a medium (about 4-5 developers) startup dedicated in mobile iOS and Android apps, due to my hunger for knowledge and the requirements of a startup i jumped project to project. I have worked as the lead dev for an entire year on a SMPP Gateway that connected and connects to the three main Portugal mobile operators currently (Done in Erlang) Then switch to the backend of a social messing app (backed up by ejabberd XMPP server) in which i was responsible for setup, customization and development of modules to backup the messing app I then moved on to the iOS part of the app and later was responsible for the port to Android. In a nutshell i worked with Erlang for about 2 years, mnesia db, MySQL, Nitrogen, Objective-C , Android Java language. Later on i decided it was time to switch off the startup mindset of burnout and impossible deadlines and went to work in a research project for the Medicine School of UMinho University developing and maintain the frontend (Android) and backend (.Net Framework Web Api backed up with SQL Server 2000) of an app related MyEnergy related to the monitoring and supervision of health patients conditioned by certain specific diseases such as diabetes with the assistance of a set of rules, formulas and guidance's provided by my fellow Nutritionist researcher). In the meanwhile i was invited to develop a mobile android and ios application which allows Med School students, teachers and staff to consult their schedules, school activities, sign up for activities, download classes attachments and receive/send notifications related to calendar activitys and teacher provided information. Later i was invited to stay as part of the Medical School programming team staff which provides custom software (the called intranet portal) with large specter of functionaly from student and staff management, to calendar and grade evaluation, mailing list, researcher catalog, teacher evaluation and so on.

At present I have advanced knowledge of 5+ programming languages (JavaScript, Node.js, PHP, Perl, ASP, Visual FoxPro) and related fields (OS Linux, Bash scripting, HTML, CSS, MySQL, MongoDB, Docker, Redis, RabbitMQ, Git, Nginx, Apache, etc.). Websites creation, OpenSource projects deployments, applications development, building, testing and improvement - these are the number of skills every day I'm work with and in what I'm improving my abilities constantly.
